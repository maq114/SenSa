How does it work?

This is SenSa

A fully responsive magnetometer connected to the power leads of your home appliances
Monitors current-flow by detecting changes in the wire's magnetic field when appliances are turned on

Uses machine learning to estimate the power consumption of the appliances

Information fed in real time to the cloud and accessible through a dedicated mobile platform

More information

Stay on top of your energy consumption

According to The Telegraph, four million households have been overcharged for their energy consumption, with the total overpaid estimated to be £300m.

With Sensa you can keep track of your consumption, to avoid surprise bills.

Easy to install for use

Sensa is very easy to install. It clips onto the power lead of the applicances you have connected at home. The only setup needed is connecting Sensa to your WiFi network, and Sensa is ready to go.

With further developments in the smart home industry, Sensa's potential in your home is limitless.

Mobile platform interface

Sensa connects to the internet, and delivers your energy consumption stats in real-time to the cloud, which is accessible through a mobile platfrom.

It also draws tarrifs from energy providers online, in order to estimate your bill, and give you recommendations on how to save energy, as shown below.
